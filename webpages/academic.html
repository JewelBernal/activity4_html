About Me Academic Life My Family

UMS

United Montessorean School, where I studied from Pre-school to Grade 4. The campus is located in San Pedro, Laguna, near the boundary to Muntinlupa. This is where my education began, I could say that the academic track here was a little "outdated" compared to the other schools I attended. We were only studying multiplication during Grade 4 whereas we should already be studying factors already, but welp.

JRSS

Jesus the Risen Savior School, a De La Salle Accredited School. I transferred here from UMS, and studied from Grade 5 to Grade 9. When I first transferred here, I've had a hard time catching up to advanced lessons as I wasn't taught lessons like here compared to when I was studying in UMS. Here I first joined Volleyball Varsity Team. I learned a lot here, in terms of life lessons, and academic.

SMS

Southernside Montessori School, the campus is located in Muntinlupa. I transferred here after 9th Grade in JRSS. Tuition Fee here is significantly lower compared to JRSS, academic track is the same to JRSS so it was a win. I studied Grade 10 and finished Senior Highschool here. Met a lot of friends here and I continued and ended my Volleyball Varsity career here until graduation. I'll always remember my time here.

NU - Manila

National University Manila, I started college here. I took the course Bachelor of Science in Information Technology - Mobile and Web Applications. My whole time enrolled here was spent on online classes because of the pandemic, so I can't say I enjoyed my time here, nevertheless, professors and education here are okay.

iACADEMY, Nexus

iACADEMY, campus located in Makati. I didn't finish my course BSIT and shifted to Software Engineering and transferred here since Software Engineering was actually my first choice and was on the fence between choices and have to make a decision fast. Here, I haven't even finished yet, but I am really enjoying my time here. I am learning a lot, in a fun way, and in a challenging way, which I enjoy. Made few friends along too.
